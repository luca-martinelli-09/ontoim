Abstract

Questa ontologia, unita alle ontologie della rete italiana OntoPiA, permette di modellare la realtà di un Comune. Include eventi, associazioni, imprese, scuole, ospedali, aree verdi, piante, segnaletica stradale, osservazioni sul traffico, incidenti stradali e statistiche demografiche.

Accessible Point of Interestc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: https://w3id.org/ontoim/AccessiblePointOfInterest

An Accessible Point of Interest is a Point of Interest, usually a building, a shopping center, a pharmacy, etc.... It inherits the properties of OntoPiA's Point of Interest, but it also has an online or physical contact point, access conditions, offered services descriptions and an owner (Organization).
